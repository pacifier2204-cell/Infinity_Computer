About Company

A Legacy of Reliable Technical Expertise

Based in Bharuch, Gujarat, Infinity Computer has grown from a local service center into a comprehensive IT solutions firm. We specialize in providing end-to-end technology support for businesses, educational institutions, and individual professionals.

With over 14 years (Since 2010) of operational excellence, we combine industry-leading products with deep technical field knowledge to solve complex IT challenges. Our firm is built on the foundations of Integrity, Innovation, and Interconnectivity.
