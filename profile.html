About Company

A Legacy of Reliable Technical & Infrastructure Expertise

Based in Bharuch, Gujarat, Infinity Computer has evolved from a local service center into a comprehensive IT, Telecom & Digital Infrastructure solutions provider.

With over 14+ years (Since 2010) of experience, we specialize in delivering end-to-end solutions including IT hardware, networking, fiber infrastructure, surveillance systems, and enterprise support services.
