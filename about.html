About Us

Company Profile and Technology Commitment

Infinity Computer is committed to delivering reliable IT products, services, and educational solutions that support businesses and individuals with practical, future-ready technology.

Company Overview

Infinity Computer, based in Zadeshwar, Bharuch, serves clients with end-to-end IT offerings, including hardware sales, service support, surveillance systems, printing solutions, software support, and digital transformation guidance.

Our approach is grounded in quality products, honest recommendations, and responsive service delivery that aligns with customer goals.

Experience & Expertise

Our team supports both retail and business clients with multi-brand product advisory, installation assistance, repairs, annual maintenance planning, and structured support for IT operations.

  • Hardware consulting and deployment
  • CCTV and security infrastructure support
  • Printer and office device management
  • Business software and digital payment enablement

Authorized Partnerships

Infinity Computer works with recognized technology brands and partners to provide trusted products and service continuity.

Business Values

  • Integrity in every recommendation and transaction
  • Quality-led brand and service selection
  • Time-bound support and issue resolution
  • Long-term relationship building with customers
  • Continuous learning and technical upskilling

Customer Commitment

We understand that each customer has unique technology needs. Our commitment is to offer dependable guidance, practical solutions, and consistent after-sales support that helps clients stay productive and confident.
